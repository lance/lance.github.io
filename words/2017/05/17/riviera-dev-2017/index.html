Riviera Dev 2017

Wed May 17 2017

I returned late Sunday night from a trip to the south of France, where I was privileged to speak about Microservices, OpenShift, and how to do it all on Node.js. The conference was RivieraDev/JUDCon: Riviera 2017 and took place in Sophia Antipolis, near Nice, Cannes, St. Tropez and other fabulous Mediterranean cities on the southeastern coast of France. It was gorgeous.

I blogged about it over on the Red Hat Developers blog. Check it out.
